PixelFlux is a Pixel Art browser application for generating game and site resources.
Design, create, animate and export your creations
PixelFlux is still at the early stages of development. Sign up for free to get involved and help shape the future of PixelFlux. With a free account you will also be able to save your work online in future versions. Don't worry though, you can save in your local browser or download your work if you want to try it out first. This doesn't require an account.
