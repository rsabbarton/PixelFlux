PixelFlux Privacy Policy

Who we are?

Pixelflux.me is a private project created by the author alone. At present there is no business association with the website and it is entirely free to use. This does not mean that privacy is not a key part of the project and preserving user privacy is a consideration in all development decisions.

Cookies

The use of cookies is required if you login using your Google Account. Otherwise, cookies are used for site tracking and do not contain personal data.

What information is captured?

Pixelflux captures only information available in your public profile and stores only your email address and profile image URL. This is captured so that images and data created using pixelflux.me can be saved on our server and returned to from another location by logging in again with your email.

Contact Information

Pixelflux.me was built and is continuing to be developed by Richard Sabbarton. You can contact richard.sabbarton@gmail.com if you have any concerns regarding privacy.
